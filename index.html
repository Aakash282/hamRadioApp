hamRadio

hamRadio constructs a radio channel based on a seed song using a wide range of musical attributes such as tempo, loudness, energy, danceability, popularity and more. Simply create a Spotify playlist called 'hamRadio' and we'll add awesome new songs to it as you listen.

Explore music that you'll love!

With hamRadio you can find new music that matches your musical tastes on a wide range of parameters. Just follow these steps:

  1. Create a playlist called 'hamRadio' in Spotify.
  2. Add a few songs you already like.
  3. Login with your Spotify credentials
  4. Enjoy! Your playlist will be updated while you listen.
